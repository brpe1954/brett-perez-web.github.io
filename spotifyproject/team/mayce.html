Bio

When pulling my data I expected to see a lot of folk music but I was unsure who would be my top listener. I was guessing that it would either be Caamp or Zach Bryan. I also expected to see some pop in between the artists mentioned above. When I got my data back I was somewhat surprised. Taylor Swift was my top artist with over 1000 listens. While I figured she would be in the top I didn’t expect her to be number 1. Another top listened to artist I was shocked by but found kind of funny was white noise. After reviewing this data I created a spider visualization that showed the danceability, energy, and valence of my music. I also created a bar graph that showed my top 5 artists in each season. In both of these visualizations I saw a lot of similarities over each season. A lot of my top artists repeated throughout the seasons and the energy and danceability of my music was nearly the same at 0.61. I think from these visualizations someone can tell that even though the seasons change I know the type of music I like so I will hardly change what I am listening to.

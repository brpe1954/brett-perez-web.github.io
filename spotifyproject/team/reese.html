Bio

Going into this project I had expectation of who some of my top artists would be. I have a pretty consistent listening trend throught the year, but I also expected to see more unique information that I wouldnt be able to guess. After completing our analysis, I was most surprised about the quantity of listens I had for some songs, and how much more and less I listened to some songs than others. From my data I decided to build various visualizations, most notably my spider chart and bar chart. With these visualizatations, primarily the spider chart, I set out to showcase the tendencies I have when it comes to what I listen to, primarily the rate of danceability, tempo and energy. I decided to pursue this because of the perceived tendencies I have, and I wanted to see how accurate that was. For the bar chart, I wanted to visualize the rate of which I listen to my top artist and how that trend carried throughout the seasons.

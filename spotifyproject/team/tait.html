Bio

My trends were interesting but not surprising. My listening habits are pretty dominated by my top artist which I rarely stray from. What was interesting was how drastically seasonality affected who I listened to most. We see a big spike in Frank Ocean during some seasons, or Kanye in another that indicates that a certain artist dominates each season. I made spider charts and bar charts to visualize overall key sonic components of the music I listen to, and how my top artists' streams changed season to season, respectively. My story to tell is that my top 5 are consistent but their relative position changes season to season pretty drastically.
